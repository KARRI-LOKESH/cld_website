Eligibility Criteria

Undergraduate Programs: 10+2 with a minimum of 50% marks.

Postgraduate Programs: Bachelor's degree with a minimum of 55% marks.

Admission Process

  1. Fill the online application form.
  2. Upload required documents.
  3. Appear for the entrance examination.
  4. Check your results and proceed with counseling.
  5. Confirm admission and pay the fees.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We offer various scholarships and financial aid programs for deserving students:

  • Merit-based scholarships for top-performing students.
  • Need-based financial aid for economically weaker sections.
  • Government-funded scholarships for eligible candidates.
  • Special scholarships for female students and differently-abled students.
